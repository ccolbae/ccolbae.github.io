Doug

Meet Doug, the roundest, fluffiest bundle of joy this side of the Howling Abyss! Doug is a curious little poro with a heart as big as his mustache. He loves belly flops in fresh snow, chasing after snowflakes, and warming up by the campfire (preferably in someone’s lap).

Though he’s a bit shy at first, offer him a Poro-Snax and you’ll have a friend for life.

Doug’s dream? To find a cozy home with someone who gives great cuddles and doesn't mind a little snow on the rug.
