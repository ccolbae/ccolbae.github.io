Biscuit

Say hello to Biscuit, the coziest little poro you’ll ever meet! With fur as soft as fresh snow and eyes full of wonder, Biscuit is a sweet-natured snuggler who waddles through the tundra leaving tiny heart-shaped hoofprints behind. He loves curling up in mittens, snoozing during snowstorms, and sneaking treats when no one’s looking.

Biscuit may be a bit of a goof, but he’s loyal to the core and always ready to cheer you up with a happy blep and a fluffy headbutt. He’s looking for a warm-hearted adventurer to share snowy strolls and snack breaks.
